Got Questions

Before You Book

How do I book a session?

+
Fill the contact form below or DM us on Instagram. We reply within 24 hours to discuss your concept and find a date.

How much does a tattoo cost?

+
Custom work starts at $150. Final price depends on size, detail, and placement. Full quote given after your free consultation.

Does it hurt?

+
Honestly, yes — some spots more than others. Our artists work efficiently and check in throughout. Most clients say it was easier than they expected.

How do I prepare for my appointment?

+
Eat well before you come, stay hydrated, and wear clothing that allows easy access to the area. No alcohol 24 hours before.

How long does healing take?

+
Surface healing takes 2 to 3 weeks. Full healing is 3 to 4 months. We give detailed aftercare instructions after every session.

Do you do cover-ups?

+
Yes, and it is one of our strengths. We assess the existing work in a free consultation and design something that transforms it completely.
